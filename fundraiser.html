Dine & Donate

Partner with Bakudan Ramen for your next fundraiser. Great food, great cause, great community.

Fundraiser Program

FEED YOUR COMMUNITY.
FUEL YOUR CAUSE.

Bakudan Ramen is proud to support San Antonio’s schools, sports teams, churches, and nonprofits through our Dine & Donate fundraiser program. It’s simple, free to participate, and makes a real difference.

HOW IT WORKS

📅

1. PICK A DATE

Choose a fundraiser night at any of our three locations. We recommend Monday through Wednesday for best availability.

📣

2. SPREAD THE WORD

We’ll provide a digital flyer for your organization to share. Participants bring the flyer or mention your group when ordering.

🍜

3. DINE IN OR ORDER

Your supporters enjoy a delicious meal — dine-in or online ordering both count toward the fundraiser total.

💰

4. WE DONATE BACK

Bakudan Ramen donates 15–20% of qualifying sales directly to your organization. It’s that simple.

15–20%

OF QUALIFYING SALES DONATED BACK

No upfront cost. No minimum. No risk to your organization. Just bring your supporters in, and we’ll write the check.

WHO CAN PARTICIPATE?

Our Dine & Donate program is open to any community organization in the greater San Antonio area. We especially love working with:

🏫 SCHOOLS & PTAs

Elementary, middle, and high schools within the San Antonio area, along with their PTA and parent councils. A fun alternative to bake sales and car washes.

⚽ YOUTH SPORTS LEAGUES

Soccer, baseball, basketball, cheer, swim teams, and more. Help fund uniforms, equipment, tournament fees, and travel with a team dinner night.

⛪ CHURCHES & FAITH GROUPS

Churches, faith-based organizations, youth ministry groups, and community outreach programs. Great for fellowship and fundraising in one evening.

🎓 UNIVERSITY ORGANIZATIONS

UTSA student clubs, Greek organizations, honor societies, and campus groups. Raise funds for events, philanthropy, or chapter activities.

💙 SCOUTS & YOUTH GROUPS

Boy Scouts, Girl Scouts, 4-H clubs, and other youth development organizations. An easy and delicious way to support troop activities and projects.

🎭 NONPROFITS & ARTS

Local nonprofits, food banks, shelters, community centers, dance studios, and performing arts groups. We’re here to help you make a difference.
