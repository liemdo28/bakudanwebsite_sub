About This Location

Our Stone Oak location brings Bakudan’s bold ramen to one of San Antonio’s most popular neighborhoods. Situated along Highway 281 near Stone Oak Parkway, it’s the perfect stop for families, professionals, and ramen lovers in the north San Antonio area.

Whether you’re swinging by after shopping at The Vineyard, catching a quick lunch break, or treating the family to dinner, our Stone Oak location offers the same incredible bowls and welcoming atmosphere. Easy access from 281 with ample parking.

What’s on the Menu

Every Bakudan location serves our full menu of slow-simmered ramen bowls including Garlic Tonkotsu, Spicy Miso, and Vegetarian Shoyu, plus appetizers, drinks, and daily Happy Hour specials from 3–6 PM.
