PRIVACY POLICY

Last updated: February 2026

Introduction

At Bakudan Ramen ("we," "us," "our," or "Company"), we are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring transparency about how we collect, use, and protect your information. This Privacy Policy explains our data practices when you visit our website, place orders, or interact with our services.

Information We Collect

Cookies

We use cookies to enhance your browsing experience. Essential cookies enable core functionality like navigation and user preference management. Optional analytics cookies help us understand how visitors use our site. You can manage your cookie preferences through our cookie consent banner.

Online Ordering Data

When you place orders through our Toast online ordering system, your order information (including name, address, phone number, and order details) is collected directly by Toast. Please review Toast's privacy policy for details on how they handle this data. We do not store your payment information.

Contact Form Submissions

If you submit information through our contact form, we collect the information you provide (name, email, message) to respond to your inquiry. This data is used solely for communication purposes and is not shared with third parties.

How We Use Your Information

We use collected information to:

  • Process and fulfill your orders through Toast
  • Respond to customer inquiries and provide customer support
  • Analyze website usage patterns and improve user experience
  • Comply with legal obligations
  • Ensure website security and prevent fraud

Third-Party Services

Toast

We use Toast for online ordering. When you place an order, your information is processed by Toast according to their privacy policies and terms of service. Toast is responsible for securing payment and order information.

Google Maps

We use Google Maps to display our restaurant locations. Google may collect data about your interactions with maps. Please review Google's privacy policy for details.

Google Fonts

Our website uses Google Fonts for typography. Google may collect limited data about font requests. This does not involve personal identification.

Possible Future Analytics

We may implement analytics services in the future to better understand website usage. We will update this policy and obtain appropriate consent before collecting such data.

Cookies and Tracking

Essential Cookies

Essential cookies are necessary for the website to function properly. They enable core features like navigation, page loading, and security. These cookies are set automatically and you cannot opt out while still accessing our site.

Optional Analytics Cookies

With your consent, we may use analytics cookies to understand how visitors interact with our website. This helps us improve content, features, and user experience. You can decline analytics cookies through our cookie consent banner without affecting basic site functionality.

Your Privacy Rights

California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA) Rights

If you are a California resident or a resident accessing our site from California, you have the following rights under the CCPA:

  • Right to Know: You have the right to request what personal information we have collected about you
  • Right to Delete: You have the right to request deletion of personal information we have collected from you (subject to certain exceptions)
  • Right to Opt-Out: You have the right to opt out of the sale or sharing of your personal information

To exercise these rights, please contact us at info@bakudanramen.com. We will verify your request and respond within 45 days as required by law.

Texas Data Privacy and Security Act (TDPSA) Rights

If you are a Texas resident, the Texas Data Privacy and Security Act (effective July 2024) provides you with the following rights regarding your personal data:

  • Right to Access: You may request confirmation of whether we process your personal data and access to that data.
  • Right to Correct: You may request correction of inaccurate personal data we hold about you.
  • Right to Delete: You may request deletion of your personal data (subject to certain legal exceptions).
  • Right to Data Portability: You may request a copy of your personal data in a portable, readily usable format.
  • Right to Opt-Out of Targeted Advertising: You may opt out of the processing of your personal data for targeted advertising purposes.
  • Right to Opt-Out of Sale of Data: You may opt out of the sale of your personal data to third parties.
  • Right to Opt-Out of Profiling: You may opt out of profiling in furtherance of decisions that produce legal or similarly significant effects.

To exercise any of these rights, please contact us at info@bakudanramen.com or call (210) 277-7740. We will respond to verified requests within 45 days as required by law.

Do Not Sell or Share My Personal Information

Bakudan Ramen does not sell, rent, or share your personal information with third parties for their direct marketing purposes. If you believe your data has been sold or shared without your consent, please contact us immediately at info@bakudanramen.com.

Data Security

We implement reasonable security measures to protect your information from unauthorized access, alteration, and disclosure. However, no method of transmission over the internet or electronic storage is completely secure. We cannot guarantee absolute security, but we work diligently to protect your data.

Children's Privacy

Our website is not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If we learn that we have collected personal information from a child under 13 without parental consent, we will delete such information promptly.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time to reflect changes in our practices or applicable laws. We will notify you of any material changes by posting the updated policy on our website and updating the "Last updated" date above. Your continued use of our website following any changes constitutes your acceptance of the updated Privacy Policy.
