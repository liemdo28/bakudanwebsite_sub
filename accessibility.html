ACCESSIBILITY STATEMENT

Last updated: February 2026

Our Commitment to Accessibility

At Bakudan Ramen, we are committed to providing an accessible and inclusive web experience for all visitors, including people with disabilities. We strive to conform to the Web Content Accessibility Guidelines (WCAG) 2.1 Level AA standards and continuously work to improve the accessibility of our website.

Accessibility Features

Our website includes the following accessibility features:

  • Skip Navigation Link: A skip-to-main-content link at the top of every page allows keyboard users to jump directly to the main content without tabbing through navigation.
  • Semantic HTML: Our website uses semantic HTML markup to provide proper structure and meaning to content, making it easier for screen readers to interpret.
  • ARIA Labels: We use ARIA (Accessible Rich Internet Applications) labels and attributes to enhance content accessibility for assistive technologies.
  • Keyboard Navigation: All interactive elements can be accessed and activated using keyboard controls alone, without requiring a mouse.
  • Focus Indicators: Visual focus indicators are present on all interactive elements to help keyboard users track their position on the page.
  • Color Contrast Compliance: Text and visual elements meet WCAG AA standards for color contrast ratios to support users with low vision or color blindness.
  • Responsive Design: Our website is fully responsive and adapts to different screen sizes, supporting a variety of devices and assistive technologies.
  • Reduced Motion Support: We respect user preferences for reduced motion by limiting animations for users who have enabled this preference in their operating system.

Known Limitations

We are committed to maintaining and improving accessibility. One area we are actively working to enhance is the replacement of placeholder images with properly described images and alt text. Images currently marked with decorative emoji or generic descriptions will be replaced with high-quality images and detailed alternative text that conveys the same information and purpose. This improvement will be implemented as image assets are finalized.

Third-Party Content

Please note that our website integrates with Toast, a third-party online ordering platform. While we strive to ensure our website is accessible, we have limited control over Toast's accessibility features. If you experience accessibility issues when using the Toast ordering system, we encourage you to contact us directly, and we will do our best to assist you with your order.
